About Joanna

A Global Icon
Built on Substance

Born in Warsaw, Poland and raised in Chicago, Joanna Krupa moved to Los Angeles at 20 with nothing but ambition — and became one of the most recognizable faces in international modeling and television.

With over 120 magazine covers worldwide — including Maxim, FHM, Cosmopolitan, and Shape — Joanna built a global audience that spans the US, Europe, and beyond. Her dual American-Polish identity gives brands access to two of the world's most valuable consumer markets simultaneously.

As longtime host and head judge of Poland's Top Model since 2010, she remains one of the most influential figures in European fashion media, while maintaining a powerful US presence through Real Housewives of Miami, Dancing with the Stars, and an engaged social following.

Fighting for
Every Animal
Every Day

DIOZ — the Lower Silesian Animal Protection Inspectorate — is Poland's most decorated animal rescue organization. Each day, DIOZ inspectors stand face to face with abusers to rescue victims of cruelty, neglect, and exploitation.

Operating entirely on donations with no state funding, DIOZ runs two of the most advanced animal rescue centers in Poland — and saves thousands of animals every year. Joanna Krupa is proud to stand alongside this mission.
